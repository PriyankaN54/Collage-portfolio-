About VTU CPGS College

"VTU CPGS Kalaburagi stands as a beacon of academic excellence, dedicated to nurturing future engineers and scholars. As a premier engineering college and PG study center, we provide an environment where students are equipped with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in their respective fields. Our focus on high-quality education, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a dedicated faculty ensures that students receive a comprehensive learning experience. Along with a strong emphasis on practical skills, VTU CPGS Kalaburagi boasts impressive placement records, with top-tier companies regularly recruiting our graduates. The institute's commitment to academic and professional development makes it a standout choice for aspiring engineers and postgraduates.".

Vision and Mission

Vision

To become an outstanding Technological University at the cutting edge of Science and Technology that produces world class Knowledge-delivery, Research, Extension and Leadership in Technology innovation for Industry and Society..

Mission

  • At Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), our mission is to provide our students with a world-class education and prepare them to be leaders in their respective fields. We are committed to offering innovative programs, conducting cutting-edge research, and providing our students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the global marketplace.

  • We believe in the importance of honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all aspects of our work. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of professional and academic integrity, and we expect the same from our students, faculty, and staff.

  • We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive community that welcomes and supports people from all backgrounds and walks of life. We believe that diversity enriches our educational experience and fosters a culture of creativity, innovation, and understanding

  • At VTU, our mission and values guide everything we do, from our academic programs to our research initiatives to the way we interact with our students and community. We are proud of our commitment to excellence, integrity, and inclusivity, and we look forward to continuing to serve our students and community with distinction and excellence.
